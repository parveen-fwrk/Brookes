Asset Strategies

Stabilized: focuses on acquiring assets with greater than 90% occupancy, turn-key products, and new or nearly renovated assets; we still like to strive to increase operational efficiencies with stabilized assets.

Core-Plus: lower-tiered, trophy assets with locations near main thoroughfares and shopping, this strategy lowest amount of risk during the recovery phase of the market cycle.

Value-Add: focus is on acquiring properties with deficiencies and reinvestment through capital expenditures (capex) and improved amenities.

Opportunistic: focus is on a patient approach to turning around a deeply troubled asset, we keep large stockpiles of operational reserves on-hand during opportunistic ventures to ensure we can ride out the market and effectively reposition the asset.
