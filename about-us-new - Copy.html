About Us

Brookes is a boutique multifamily and student housing acquirers and operators in the mid-Atlantic southeastern United States.

Contact Us

Principle – Community

Our focus is to enter submarkets where there is a clear and present opportunity to improve the livelihood of our residents and the immediate surrounding community.

Principle – Safety

In all of our multifamily communities and student housing developments we strive to provide the safest communities for families and our resident students by building local partnerships.

Principle – Affordability

In a world with rising inflation with little or no wage growth and continually increasing tuition and book costs, we target investment assets that offer affordable and accessible living at median income levels.

Principle – Discipline

As United States has exhibited unparalleled growth over the last ten years plus, we seek to find recessionary-proof opportunities in middle-class multifamily and student housing assets.

Principle – Reliable Service

Safety and affordability can go a long way to help our residents, but the customer service and reliability of our property management partners helps us to differentiate our assets.

Principle – Integrity

We put the satisfaction of our investors and residents before our own and could never imagine a time or generation where that would not be our top priority.

BROOKES | Building with Our Partners

Creating a Strategic Vision

Founded in 2018, Brookes has a clear and strategic vision for the future of commercial real estate investing through the targeting of dynamic submarkets, property selection, acquisition process, investor relations, and asset management.

Our Investors

Paramount to the success at Brookes has been our longstanding private investor, family office and private equity relationships. We make the investment experience easy for our investors, first by seeking opportunities with our investment hurdles and then only selecting investment projects that are most suitable for our investors.

Our Assets

Brookes’ assets are bought with the long-term in mind beginning even before acquisition. As we try to shape our assets around communities, we want to be viewed as a trusted, long-term owner for all residents and environments. We make sure all multifamily asset offerings are attuned to the millennial generation and our student housing offers abreast of the preferences of Gen-Z.

Our Partners

We like to work with all accredited investors, strong capital raising partners, deal sponsors, and sourcers of investment opportunities. We can always find synergies and use our expertise to help, please contact info@brookesdevelopment.com.
